How Our App Works:

  • Register with all the needed information in order for first responders to find you quickly.
    • *It should be noted that people with location will be responded to first, as responders are more certain of their location.
  • If we don't receive an updated response, or your response is still "unsafe" after 15 minutes, we will upload your information you've entered on our website for first responders to find you.

Our Purpose

Our app and website strives to bring help to communities experiencing natural disasters. We want to be able to locate everyone and make sure they're safe. If not, we want first responders to be able to send help to those in need in the fastest way possible.
